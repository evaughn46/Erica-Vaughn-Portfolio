Extending Dynamics 365 with Azure Synapse Link + Databricks

Microsoft-first architecture that expands retention and speeds up analytics while keeping Power BI and Azure at the core.

Overview

I led a POC to extend Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations (F&O) reporting beyond BYOD limits using Azure-native services—Synapse Link, ADLS Gen2, and Power BI—with Databricks as a performance accelerator for retention and transformations.

Business Challenge

  • Longer historical retention was required without burdening F&O.
  • Refresh latency needed improvement as data volumes grew.
  • Maintain continuity with existing Power BI dashboards.

Architecture

Synapse Link replicated F&O tables to ADLS Gen2 in near real-time. Databricks curated Delta tables (partitioning, retention policies), and Power BI connected for analytics. Governance via Purview/RBAC.

Impact

  • Extended history with cost-efficient storage in ADLS/Delta.
  • ~30% faster refresh times vs prior approach.
  • Kept the Microsoft stack central while adding scalable transformation.

Key Takeaway

This showed how Azure-native services + Databricks can maximize Dynamics 365 data value—without compromising Microsoft-first strategy.
